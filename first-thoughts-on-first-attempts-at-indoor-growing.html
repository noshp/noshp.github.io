First Thoughts on First Attempts at Indoor Growing

I started trying to grow Basil and Spinach in my tiny condo in Feb 2018.
My relevant experience included some brief work in a greenhouse in Maine, and another in Montreal. Neither really taught me what I need to know for these experiments.
The goals include:

  • Grow greens to grab gratuitously
  • Dont develop dirt dependance
  • Automate anything arduous or annoying

In other words:
The dream is to have a self growing, dirt-free, indoor garden.

So far...
  • I still need to tweak my growing method, specifically the amount of lighting, the temperature at germination for my spinach, and the nutrients I give to older seedlings
  • I also want to track variables like temp and humidity to learn about my grow space
  • If I start growing in a tub, I would like to automate a pump to refill it when its levels get low... or at least have something tell me the levels are low
  • Finally, I would like to put a camera over the plants and track NDVI over time... this is the tallest ask
The big dream is to make a mini greenhouse that I would build this summer and use to grow cold-weather plants in the winter out on my patio in the winter.
